# Privacy Policy — CourseBell

**Effective date:** February 16, 2026

This policy describes how CourseBell handles your information.

## Data Collection

CourseBell does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All schedule data, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage and are never sent to any server.

## Notifications

CourseBell uses local notifications to alert you about bell schedules. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

## Camera

CourseBell requests camera access solely to scan QR codes for importing schedules. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

CourseBell does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

CourseBell does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/spearssoftware/coursebell/issues).
